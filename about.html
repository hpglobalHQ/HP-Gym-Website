Our story

12 Years of Transforming Lives

Founded in 2014 by Himanshu Pandey, HP Gym began as a locally owned training space for people who wanted stronger bodies and better habits. Today, it is a trusted fitness community in Jind where beginners, athletes, students, and working professionals train side by side.

Our mission is simple: deliver honest coaching, practical programming, and a clean environment where consistency becomes a lifestyle.

  1. 2014HP Gym opens near Bus Stand with a focus on strength training.
  2. 2017Group classes and personal training programs are introduced.
  3. 2020Facility expands with functional turf, cardio, and recovery zones.
  4. 2023HP Gym crosses 500 active members and launches advanced body analysis.
