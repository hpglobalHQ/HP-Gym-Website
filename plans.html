Membership FAQ

Can I try HP Gym before joining?

Yes. Visit the front desk to schedule a guided tour and trial workout slot.

Are there joining or cancellation fees?

There are no hidden cancellation fees. Any joining offers are explained clearly before payment.

Do beginners get help with equipment?

Absolutely. Trainers introduce machines, form basics, and beginner-friendly routines.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes. You can upgrade from Basic to Pro or Elite at any time by contacting reception.

Is personal training included in every plan?

Personal training starts with Pro. Elite members receive additional coaching and analysis.
